I have developed a personal blog page using Angular for frontend and Firebase for backend.

Here's a quick overview:

🔐Auth & Database: Managed using Firebase Auth and Cloud Firestore.

📝Dashboard: Includes an inbuilt editor for creating & editing posts directly from within the dashboard.

⌚Realtime Updates: View counts, posts, and comments update in real-time.

💡Category Filters: Users can easily find posts by their preferred topics.

🌟Featured Posts: Showcasing my latest or best posts.
